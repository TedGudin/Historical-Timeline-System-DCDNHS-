DCDNHS Intramurals 2023

Intramurals happens annually during the 3rd week of November. This event serves as a platform for students from various grades and classes to engage in a diverse range of sports and activities. The week-long celebration not only promotes physical fitness but also emphasizes teamwork, sportsmanship, and school spirit. It has become a cherished tradition, eagerly anticipated by students and faculty alike, enhancing the overall educational experience at DCDNHS.
